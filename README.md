# Lead Intelligence Agent

An AI‑assisted lead research and outreach tool that turns a company name/domain + target persona into a structured lead profile and a ready‑to‑send outreach draft.

**What It Can Do**
- Scrape a company website to extract a quick summary and key signals.
- Generate likely decision‑maker searches based on your ICP persona.
- Infer a light tech stack from site headers and page content.
- Produce a pain‑hypothesis tailored to the company and persona.
- Draft a personalized cold email (subject, body, CTA).
- Score the lead with a simple reply probability and reasoning.

**Why It’s Important**
- Sales and marketing teams lose time on manual research.
- Outreach without context leads to low response rates.
- Prioritization is hard without a consistent scoring lens.

This tool reduces research time and gives you a consistent, structured output for every lead.

**Real‑World Problems It Solves**
- “I can’t quickly understand a company before outreach.”
- “My emails feel generic and don’t get replies.”
- “I don’t know which leads to prioritize first.”

**Who It’s For**
- SEO agencies and growth teams doing outbound.
- B2B SaaS teams building a pipeline.
- SDRs and founders who need fast, repeatable research.
